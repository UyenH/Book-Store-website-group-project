Our Vision

To create a world where literacy and creativity become second nature to all people.

Our Mission

To provide affordable and convinient educational resources such as books to all people.

Our Values

We value curiosity since it is the fuel that encourages people to learn new knowledge and share it with people. We value imagination since it gives people the freedom to create their own stories. Lastly We value collaboration since the greatest inovations are forged from groups of people.
